Our Products

MANAGEMENT PLATFORM

Full event registration, guest communication and post-event analytic solution. All our products are managed from this platform and all data acquired flows back into the Event Management System.

 

    • RSVP
      • Customized registration platform (Branded)
      • Browser-based
      • PayPal Payment systems available
      • Ticketing & seating functionality
      • Single or series of events
      • Customizable in Letsface CMS-system
      • Confirmation SMS or e-mails option
    • EMS
      • CRM Database for events
      • Provides overview of gathered data
      • 'Live' analytics for better understanding of user dynamics
      • Data Export & Import functionality
      • Integrated with all Letsface Products
    • Check-in
      • Our friendly hostesses assist guests to digitally check-in on tablets.
      • Interactive, fun & fast/smooth check-in process with speeds of 2,000 visitors p/hr.
      • Fully branded according to client KV.
      • Available for events of up to 20,000 visitors.
      • Tablet guest lists instantly up-to-date via Letsface EMS.
      • Photo collections of visitors at event.
      • Search for guests using name, email or mobile.
      • Compatible with ALL other Letsface Systems.
      • No paperwork.
    • Printing
      • On-site, instant printing of badges
      • Based on tablet check-in information.
      • In-sync with Letsface EMS
      • Print: Guest names, function, check-in pictures, company names/logo's, QR-codes, bar-codes, etc.
    • Survey
      • On-site, instant survey at check-in.
      • Based on tablet check-in information.
      • In-sync with Letsface EMS
      • Real-Time statistics from gathered data.
    • Analytics

ENGAGEMENT PRODUCTS

Real-Time, fun visualization of guests’ attendance, contribution and their likes on any digital media – Mobile Phone, Tablets, Interactive Touch Screens, Projections, LED and 3D mapping.

 

  • FaceWall
    • Guests are welcomed by hostess with tablets
    • Guests are checked-in with tablets
    • The check-in picture appears on LED or Projection Wall.
    • Suitable for interactive events, trade fairs, exhibitions
    • Graphically adjusted to KV of client (incl. video/animation).
  • PhotoWall
    • Pictures that are taken during the event appear on projection/LED walls.
    • Suitable for interactive events, trade fairs, exhibitions.
    • Graphically adjusted to KV of client (incl. video/animation).
  • SignatureWall
    • Guests sign their signature on tablets presented by hostess.
    • Signatures instantly appear on SignatureWall.
    • Graphically adjusted to KV of client (incl. video/animation).
    • Signature forming logo/brand
  • PhotoScreen
    • Professional Photographer takes pictures during event & transmits Letsface 'PhotoScreen' using WiFi.
    • Guests can select up to 9 photos each time on touch screen to share their pictures through email, Weibo & Wechat. Onsite printing is also possible: guests can select up to 3 photos each time for printing, client's logo can be printed on each photo.
  • Digital Sketch
    • Our digital artists make DigitalSketch for the guest on a tablet.
    • The Sketches appear on the PhotoScreen for sharing or printing.
    • The Sketches appear on PhotoWall for every guest to view
  • Games
    • Compatible with check-in system, and pictures can be used.
    • Custom games can be developed.
    • Branded games on large interactive touch-screen LCD Screens.
    • Quiz-questions can be included to declare winners.
  • Lucky Draw
    • User friendly control panel interface
    • Customizable for different rounds, prizes, background music, winner's music, number of winners per round, and timeslot selection
    • Additional guest removal feature
    • Visualization of the winners *
  • Voting
    • Guests can vote on presented topics.
    • Our tablets are set up for sharing, or single guest use.
    • Voting application can be customized according to client KV. Results can be shown live or after the event using animated visuals (adjusted for KV), using tv's, LED-walls or Projection.
